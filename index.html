CHINESE GARDEN


ABOUT

Chinese Garden is the place to go to escape from the bustling city. Located in the west, Chinese Garden has eye-catching
architectures and breathtaking views. It is a great place for history buffs and nature lovers to explore.
Head on down with your family or significant other to get a first-hand experience! No entry fee required.

HISTORY

Designed by Taiwanese architect Prof. Yuen-chen Yu and built in 1975,
the space is modelled after the northern Chinese imperial style of architecture and landscaping, particularly during the Sung dynasty period.


PLACES TO VISIT


  • Twin Pagoda

  • Bonsai Garden

  • Moon Lantern Terrace

  • Nature Rambling Trail

Twin Pagoda

Overlooking the lake of the Chinese Garden are Twin Pagodas, modelled after architectural styles
that date back to the 5th century. Ascend the spiral staircases of the Twin Pagodas, and uncover a breathtaking vista
overlooking the tranquil lake of the Chinese Garden.

Bonsai Garden

Be transported outside of Singapore! The Bonsai Garden houses a collection of over a hundred beautifully-manicured
bonsais imported from China, Japan and neighbouring countries like Malaysia.
